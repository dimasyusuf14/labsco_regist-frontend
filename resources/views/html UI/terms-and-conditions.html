TERMS AND CONDITIONS

  1. Registration:
    • Participants must register through the official Labsco Sport website.
    • Registration is considered valid after payment is received and confirmed by the committee.
  2. Participant Eligibility:
    • Participants must be at least 18 years old on the day of the event.
    • Participants under 18 years old must have written permission from parents or guardians.
  3. Cancellation and Refund:
    • Registration cancellation can only be done before a certain date set by the committee.
    • Registration fees cannot be refunded after the cancellation deadline.
  4. Schedule Changes:
    • The committee has the right to change the event schedule due to weather or other emergency reasons.
    • Participants will be notified via email or official Labsco Sport social media.
  5. Participant Obligations:
    • Participants must comply with all rules and regulations set by the committee.
    • Participants are responsible for bringing personal equipment needed during the event.
  6. Insurance:
    • The committee is not responsible for injuries, accidents, or loss of property that occur during the event.
    • Participants are advised to have personal insurance.
  7. Copyright:
    • The committee has the right to use photos and videos taken during the event for promotional purposes without additional compensation.

RUNNING RULES

  1. Start and Finish:
    • Participants must start running from the designated start point and finish at the finish point.
    • Start and finish times will be recorded by the committee.
  2. Running Equipment:
    • Participants must wear the bib number provided by the committee throughout the entire event.
    • Participants are advised to wear clothing and shoes appropriate for the weather conditions and running route.
  3. Running Route:
    • Participants must follow the running route designated by the committee.
    • Leaving the running route without committee permission will result in disqualification.
  4. Water Stations:
    • Water stations will be provided at several points along the running route.
    • Participants are advised to stay well hydrated during the run.
  5. Health Examination:
    • Participants must ensure they are in good health before participating in the event.
    • The committee has the right to stop participants from running if deemed physically unfit.
  6. Running Ethics:
    • Participants are expected to be sportsmanlike and respect other participants.
    • Participants must not block or disturb other participants during the run.
  7. Safety:
    • Participants must comply with all safety instructions given by the committee.
    • In case of emergency, participants must immediately contact the committee or medical personnel on duty.
